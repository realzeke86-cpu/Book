For Lee Anne Landicho

A heartfelt confession from someone who admires you

By: Jayson

Dear Lee Anne,

I hope you're doing well. I just wanted to tell you something and I thought the best way would be through something as special as you are.

Do you remember when we first connected through your TikTok live? I was just another viewer in the crowd, but something about your presence made me want to know you better.

At first, I was hesitant. How do you tell someone you've never really talked to that they've captured your attention? But the more I watched your lives, the more I realized that you're someone genuinely worth knowing.

Your username became something I'd look for every time I opened TikTok, my day's has become even more exciting because of you and I was genuinely happy that I have met you.

1

Getting to Know You

Since then, I've found myself tuning in whenever you go live. Your smile and the way you interact with everyone, it all drew me in. I looked forward to those moments, even if I was just quietly watching from the other side of the screen.

And then I saw you in person. I was nervous, but the moment I did, everything I felt was confirmed. You're not just pretty on camera. You're genuinely beautiful, inside and out. Your angelic smile, your decent and kind demeanor, it all made my heart skip a beat.

There's something about the way you carry yourself that makes you stand out. You're kind to everyone, always respectful, and you have this grace that's so rare to find these days.

Every time I see you, whether it's on screen or in person, I can't help but admire not just your beauty, but the person you are. You've become someone I genuinely care about.

2

My Confession

I created all this for you, because I just wanted to say that I have a crush on you. It's more than just admiration. It's the kind of feeling that makes me smile when I think of you, that makes me hope you're doing well, that makes me want to know you even more.

I don't know what the future holds, but I know that I had to be honest with you. You deserve to know how someone feels about you, especially when those feelings are as genuine as mine.

With admiration and sincerity,
Jayson

3

~ The End ~
